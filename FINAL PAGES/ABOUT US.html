ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE


VICLYN TRAVEL AND TOURS AGENCY is a uganda based eco-tourism and travel company
That is situated in the western part of the country in FORT PORTAL TOURISM CITY. The agency and its staff is so passionate about nature and its conservation
The western region is richly endored with a beauty landscape rain forests,wildlife,waterbodies .


The knowledge and experience required to deliver such high-quality service come from our years of dedicated industry service and our love of Africa, its beauty, and the joy that comes from helping others share that experience. That dedication and true love of the safari industry drives us on every day to exceed our client’s expectations and deliver more to make their trip as special and memorable as it can be tour of pure relaxation, cultural tours, safari and trekking tours, weekend trips or special packages that combine culture and safari together, we can deliver the perfect tour for every client and ensure that memories of Africa are always great ones.

WHY US?

Our service has provided a benchmark for those looking to experience the true freedom of travel while having the security of constant, but discreet assistance from knowledgeable and experienced staff when required. Through the hard work and enthusiasm of our team, we are proud to have the trust of an ever-growing, exclusive and highly demanding customer base to deliver the exceptional travel solutions that meet those expectations.


OUR SERVICES:


We offer quality, affordable, deluxe, and luxury Primate adventure tours like Gorilla trekking/habituation, chimpanzee tracking/habituation, Rwenzori mountain treks and peak climbing, National parks safaris, white water rafting experiences, Cultural and community tour, Bird watching, honeymoon vocations, Hotel and airport transfers.
Our guides are certified Ugandan experts, English speakers, friendly, hospitable and reliable to our clients

NOTE:

For non English speaking clients, don’t worry we have linguistic expert guides on a request.

Being experienced local based tour company, and Versed in the flora and fauna of the region, we know how to work and support the local community because we are born here; we live and raise our families here. When you travel with us, your support creates strong harmony between people and wildlife conservation.
