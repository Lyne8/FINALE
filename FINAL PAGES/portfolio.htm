Murchison Falls National Park

Murchison Falls National Park is Uganda's largest national park and offers spectacular views of the Nile River, wildlife safaris, and boat cruises.

Highlights:

  • Game drives to spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and more
  • Boat cruise to the base of Murchison Falls
  • Birdwatching along the Nile

Queen Elizabeth National Park

Queen Elizabeth National Park is renowned for its diverse ecosystems, including savanna, forests, and wetlands, and is home to a wide variety of wildlife.

Highlights:

  • Boat cruise on the Kazinga Channel
  • Game drives in the Kasenyi Plains
  • Chimpanzee tracking in the Kyambura Gorge

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park is famous for its population of endangered mountain gorillas, offering visitors a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for gorilla trekking.

Highlights:

  • Gorilla trekking in the lush forest
  • Birdwatching with over 350 bird species
  • Cultural experiences with local communities
