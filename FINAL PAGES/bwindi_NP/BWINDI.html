A Guide to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park & Neighboring Areas

A Complete Guide to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park with travel information on what to do,
where to go, things to see, maps & guides, where to stay, tips & advice.
This travel guide is packed with complete travel information to help you plan a gorilla safari to Bwindi Forest & explore
the neighboring areas including Kigezi Highlands, Lake Mutanda, Lake Bunyonyi, Echuya
Forest & more. This guide features up-to-date information on attractions, hotels, restaurants,
places where to go shopping, enjoy nightlife, travel tips and more. Rated as the No. 1
destination and vacation planner for Bwindi Forest, South Western Uganda.

This site is dedicated to making sure you have and maximize the best of your visit to Bwindi Forest
National Park by educating you in advance on all that the destination has to offer and how you
can plan a smooth safari in Uganda, the Pearl of Africa.
A trip to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park is a lifetime journey to a special protected area that
has been recognized by UNESCO as a world heritage site. Since gorilla tourism was introduced in
Bwindi in 1994, a considerable number of tourists visit the park every year. The numbers of
tourists are highly regulated and a carrying capacity is highly respected. Since Bwindi is an ecotourism destination,
it is important to plan your trip well in advance.
